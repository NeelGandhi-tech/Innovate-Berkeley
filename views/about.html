OUR MISSION

Innovate@Berkeley is one of the biggest startup conferences in the Bay Area. We aim to capture the entrepreneurial spirit of Silicon Valley and provide a platform for our community to connect, learn, and get inspired.

Meet the Organizers

Alpha Epsilon Zeta

Innovate@Berkeley is organized by the brothers of Alpha Epsilon Zeta (AEZ). AEZ distinguishes itself from professional fraternities by embracing a multidisciplinary focus, dedicated to promoting and fostering professionalism, brotherhood, unity and tradition.
